    Rankings: Explained

    Rankings in Go start at a Kyu level for beginners, then progress into amateur Dan, and finally professional Dan.

    Kyu begins at 30 kyu (also denoted as 30k) for absolute beginners and goes up to 1 kyu (1k). Once a player reaches the rank of Dan, however, they begin at 1 amateur dan (1d), and progress to 7 dan (7d). If a player is fortunate enough to progress into the professional ranking, they begin as a 1 professional dan (1p) and the only very best can progress to a 9 dan (9p).

    Beginner: 30k-20k
    Casual Player: 19k-10k
    Intermediate Amateur: 9k-1k
    Advanced Amateur: 1d-7d
    Professional Player: 1-9p
